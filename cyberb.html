Project Cyber B

Information on Project

  • THIS IS CURRENTLY A WORK IN PROGRESS GAME, WILL BE ON DISPLAY AT RIT IMAGINE ON APRIL 28, 2018.
  • This is a VR game using the HP Mixed Reality Headset.
  • The player is "entering the system" when they put up the headset. They go in and are trying to get the information in the center on the map. The system wants the player out so it is sending out waves of enemies to stop you.
  • Using your special abilities to fight off the waves of enemies and reach the end of end goal and get out.
  • Can you get in and out without dying?
  • This game is being worked on by a team of six people. Using Unity 2017.3.1p3.
