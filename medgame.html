Med Game

Information on Project

  • This was a game created in a partnership with the University of Rochester medical department.
  • Purpose of the game is to help medical students with their material. The focus of the game is about pregnancy and all the things that could happen during pregnancy.
  • I was in charge of designing the UI and creating the art assets for the game.
  • When it came to creating the UI I have to keep in mind that the people who would be playing are not familiar with games so I could not use traditional design practices.
  • There are no medical illustrator to do the art for the game so I was also incharge of creating those assets as well.
  • Adobe Photoshop 2015 was use to create the assets.
