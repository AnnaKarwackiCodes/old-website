Null Sector

Information on Project

  • This is a Tabletop RPG in the new USA under the guidance of an all knowing AI BINF.
  • The players are all apart of different groups around the capital of this new United States who all have different skill focuses and relationships with the other groups.
  • This system also features a unique reverse dice system where rolling low is your key to success.
  • This game is focused toward experienced tabletop RPG players.
  • Was created by a team of 5 people over 16 weeks.
