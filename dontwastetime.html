Don't Spend Time On It

Information on Project

This game was created over a weekend during Hero Jam Fall 2017. Created by two people: myself and Andrew Dillon. The theme of the game jam was "Everyday heros" so the theme of the game was professors and TAs working together to answer student questions in a Diner Dash meets Typing of the Dead two player game. The professor player is typing words that appear on the board while the TA is running around adding words to the professor's queue. If the word is already in the queue your points are multipled. The players are working together to get the best score they can and get that gold star. My role on this game was programming and implementing the art and animations. This game was made in Unity 5.6.3.

Won "Best Game" at Hero Jam Fall 2017. Was judged by 3 Game Design and Development professor at RIT.
