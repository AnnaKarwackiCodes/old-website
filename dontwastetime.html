Don't Spend Time On It

Information on Project

  • Was created in a weekend during the Hero Jam game jam at RIT in Fall 2017 following the theme of "Everyday Heros".
  • This was created by two people: myself and Andrew Dillon
  • Inspired by the games: Diner Dash and Typing of the Dead, Don't Spend Time On It is a two player game where one player is playing as a professor and the other is playing as the TA.
  • The professor's job is to type out the words on the dry erase board behind the profressor.
  • The TA is running around "answering" questions which adds the word to the list for the professor. If the word is up there already a multiplier starts.
  • The characters in the game are inspired by two people in my life: a professor who I've learned a lot from and a TA who has been there for me.
  • My role on this game was programming and i,plementing the art, animations, and music.
  • This game was made in Unity 5.6.3

Won "Best Game" at Hero Jam Fall 2017. Was judged by 3 Game Design and Development professor at RIT.
