If You Meme it

Information on Project

A team based project created during Global Game Jam 2017 with a team of 6 people. This is a 2 player game where you throw back and forth the "scope hammer" to fight off waves of enemies. My role on this project was to make enviromental props and to texture the props and the character models. Maya 2016 and Photoshop was used.
