Break the Mirror

Information on Project

This is an experimental game being worked on under the supervision of Ian Scrieber. This is the story of a knight facing their greatest foe, something that is not seen or felt by everyone. A level 99 knight that by no means should be facing difficulties, but is. This game is using the themes and tropes of the RPG genre and showing what it's like to suffer with depression. This game is still in the early stages of development. The first prototype of this game was make in Game Maker, currently being worked on in Unity 5.6.
