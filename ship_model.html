Ship Model

Information on Model

This was created for a solo project where we had to create and animate a model with at least three moving pieces. The animated pieces of this ship is the gun on the top, the lower wings on both the right and left side, and the landing gear on the bottom. So technically I have four moving pieces. This ship is based off the blueprints printed on the dust jacket for the book "Armada" by Ernest Cline.(Fall 2015)
