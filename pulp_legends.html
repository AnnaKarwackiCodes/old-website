Pulp Legends

Information on Project

  • One to two player platformer game inspired by pulp novels and noir movies.
  • Player(s) are running around the city fighting mobsters and reaching the scene of the crime.
  • My job on the project was themeing, character design, art asset creation and character animations.
  • This game was created with a team 4 people.
  • Unity and Photoshop were used.
