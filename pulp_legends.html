Pulp Legends

Information on Project

A team based project where we created a platformer game that is influenced by Noir Films and Pulp Novels. This game was made in 5 weeks with a team of 4 people. My role on this project was of character design and character artist. All sprite sheets and animations were done by me. Unity and Photoshop was used.
