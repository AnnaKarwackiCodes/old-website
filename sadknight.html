The Sad Knight's Quest

Information on Project

This game’s original concept was an idea given to us by our professor, Ian Schreiber, for a game to help people who are struggling through depression. There has been a rise in inclusivity among minority groups, however there aren’t many experiences that are offered for people with mental illness. Games with a focus on mental illness do exist, but are more towards the demonstrative end to help those without mental illness understand, rather than help those with it. As such we wanted to create this experience to help them continue the fight with depression. Created in Unity 5.6.
