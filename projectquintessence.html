Information on Project

Fall 2016: Large group project with 12 people making a RPG where the player makes suggestions to their allies for stances. Role on project UI/UX designer and developer. C#, Unity, and Photoshop used. This is still a work in progress project. Total timeframe of work on this project is 15 weeks

Spring 2017: This project was continued on in the spring of 2017. This time my role on the project is of enviromental artist, more specifically I created the buildings that will be used in the major city in the game. For this I created a kit that all the buildings use elements from. As of right now I have made 8 buildings.
