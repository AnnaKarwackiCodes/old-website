Anna Karwacki - Programmer - Project

Project Quintessence

Information on Project

  • Fall 2016
    • RPG where the player's interactions with other characters have an impact on what the other character will do. The player can suggest things to the other character where they may or may not listen.
    • Role on project was UI/UX developer and designer. Would put in temperary art in to help convay the feel while the artist would make the final art.
    • The team for this project was 12 people. Was developed over 15 weeks.
    • Unity, C#, and Photoshop were used.
  • Spring 2017
    • The project was continued on in the spring.
    • Role on project changed from UI/UX designer/developer to enviromental artist.
    • I created kits of building pieces that could be used to create a variety of building. Also created various enviromental props to fill the city.
    • Later populated the area with the buildings, props, and other elements created by the other artists on the team.
    • Maya and Photoshop used.
