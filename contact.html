About me

Hi! My name is Anna Karwacki and I am a currently finishing up my fourth year as a undergraduate at Rochester Institute of Technology in the Game Design and Development program. My passion is creating and when it comes to games I love doing programming and both 2D and 3D art. I know a number of tools needed to create, but if I don’t already know it, I am self-motivated and a fast learned. Life is an adventure and I’m ready to see where I go next.
